# Data notes

This visualization is an interactive reinterpretation of the “128 years of
Moore’s Law” chart assembled by Ray Kurzweil and updated by Steve Jurvetson. Its
metric is approximate computations per second per dollar—not transistor count.

The plotted values are an editorial visual transcription of the published
semi-log frontier rather than a product benchmark database. They preserve the
order of magnitude, substrate transitions, and overall 10^21 improvement that
the source chart is designed to explain. Individual values should not be used
for purchasing or benchmark comparisons.

Sources:

- Steve Jurvetson, “The Moore’s Law Update—for 128 years,” 2024, CC BY 2.0
- Computer History Museum, “1965: Moore’s Law Predicts the Future of Integrated Circuits”
- Intel, “Press Kit: Moore’s Law”
