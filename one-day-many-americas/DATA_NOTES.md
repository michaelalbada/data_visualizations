# One Day, Many Americas — data notes

The chart transcribes Bureau of Labor Statistics ATUS Table A-3 hourly estimates for the civilian noninstitutional population age 15 and over. It compares the 2024 annual estimate with the published combined 2015–19 average.

The original table's major activity categories sum to approximately 100 percent at each hour. The display combines them as follows:

- Care combines care for household and nonhousehold members.
- Home & shopping combines household activities and purchasing goods and services.
- Leisure except TV subtracts the published watching-television subset from leisure and sports.
- Other combines personal care other than sleep, telephone/mail/email, and not-elsewhere-classified activity.
- BLS includes related travel within each major activity, so the chart does not fabricate a separate travel series.

Values marked by BLS as approximately zero are transcribed as zero. The chart uses the 24 published hourly snapshots directly and does not create half-hour rhythms.
